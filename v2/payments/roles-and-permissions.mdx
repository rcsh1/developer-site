title: "Roles and permissions"
---
This document lists the user roles and their corresponding permissions involved in Cobo's payment solution.


<Note>In a production environment, you need to configure at least 2 Admins to serve as backups for each other. Other roles should be properly configured according to actual needs. It is not recommended to assign multiple roles to the same member.</Note>
